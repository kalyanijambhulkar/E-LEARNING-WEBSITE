IT Management

IT management refers to the monitoring and administration of an organization's information technology systems: hardware, software, and networks. IT management focuses on how to make information systems operate efficiently. Just as important, it's about helping people work better.

Business Management

Business management is the process of planning, organizing, directing, and controlling the activities of a business or organization to achieve its goals and objectives. It involves overseeing all aspects of a business, from finance and operations to marketing and human resources.
